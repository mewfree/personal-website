Termux (Android app)

  • Volume Down = Ctrl
  • Volume Up+Q = hides extra keys
  • Volume Up+E = Escape
  • Volume Up+T = Tab
  • Volume Up+B = Alt+B (one word backwards)
  • Volume Up+F = Alt+F (one word forward)
  • Volume Up+W/A/S/D = Up/Down/Left/Right
  • Volume Up+L = | (pipe)
  • Volume Up+H = ~ (tilde)
  • Volume Up+U = _ (underscore)

Have clipboard working with Emacs: pkg install termux-api in Termux xclip-mode in Emacs
